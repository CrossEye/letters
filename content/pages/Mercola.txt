Joseph Mercola
==============

Joseph Mercola -- an alternative medicine huckster -- is promoted in *RiverEast*
letters as a valuable source of information on subjects like Covid vaccines.

He is slicker than many of these snake-oil salesmen. But he has no more
reasonable arguments than any of them.

As is often the case, a [simple web search][dd] is a good place to start. This
should lead you to many sites, including [Wikipedia][wi] and to sites noting his
top place among the [Disinformation Dozen][ch], and include links to the New
York Times [article][ny1] *The Most Influential Spreader of Coronavirus
Misinformation Online*. (This is behind a paywall, but through October 31, 2021
there is a [freely shared][ny2] version you can read.)
